# Partners

## Consortium (6 partners, 2 countries)

| Role | Partner | Country | Contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lead partner** | **Università IUAV di Venezia** | 🇮🇹 IT | Project coordination + scientific lead. Authored the cross-border climate-change assessment, the wildfire hazard & vulnerability maps, the prevention *abaco* and the participatory laboratories. PM: Massimiliano Granceri Bradaschia. |
| Project partner | **Infordata Sistemi Srl SB** | 🇮🇹 IT | The digital platform and the fire-intelligence behind it: the KFWI, the ignition model, the daily risk map (~40,000 grid points), and cross-border alerting via email / SMS / WhatsApp. |
| Project partner | **ZRC SAZU** *(Research Centre of the Slovenian Academy of Sciences and Arts)* | 🇸🇮 SI | Co-authored the climate and hazard/vulnerability science. Built the cross-border remote-sensing burned-area database (171 historical fires from 30 years of satellite imagery). Flew four seasons of multispectral drone surveys. Leads the SI pilot. |
| Pilot municipality | **Comune di Duino Aurisina – Občina Devin Nabrežina** | 🇮🇹 IT | 9 ha of selective black-pine clearing (~270 m³ of dead wood removed), procurement of e-nose sensors and drones, reactivation of grazing on the Karst heathland, stewardship of the 107-ha Falesie di Duino reserve. |
| Pilot municipality | **Občina Miren-Kostanjevica** | 🇸🇮 SI | Ground-zero of the 2022 mega-fire (~1,000 ha burned). Co-financed three pilot actions: preventive grazing on the burn scar, dry-stone-wall restoration (firebreak + heritage), dolina mosaic restoration. Committed to scaling past 2026. |
| Project partner | **KID PiNA — Associazione Culturale ed Educativa** | 🇸🇮 SI | Designed and facilitated the participatory laboratories (~60 stakeholders), turning lived knowledge into 29 municipal + 8 national prevention measures. Gamified fire-awareness learning for young people. |
